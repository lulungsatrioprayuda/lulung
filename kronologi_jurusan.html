Information Technology

Information Technology is a department is a department in the State Polytechnic of Jember.
Same as other Departments at Jember State Polytechnic campus.
This department has the Vision, Mission and Objectives, which are as follows

Vision:
  1. As a Department that becomes the center of vocational education and the development of superior applied information technology at the Asian level in 2025.
Mission :
  1. Carry out vocational education in the field of Information Technology that is quality, innovative and competitive;
  2. Develop applied research in the field of innovative Information Technology;
  3. Carry out community service in the application of Information Technology and establish cooperation at national and international levels.
Purpose :
  1. Educate, develop students' abilities, and produce graduates who excel in knowledge and skills in the field of information technology, and are competitive, professional and ethical.
  2. Menumbuhkan iklim akademik yang kondusif yang dapat menumbuhkan sikap apresiatif, partisipatif, dan kontributif dari civitas akademika, serta menjunjung tinggi tata nilai moral akademik dalam usaha membentuk masyarakat kampus yang dinamis dan harmonis.
  3. Achieve competitive advantage through the application of the principles of resource management in accordance with the principles of professional management.
  4. Increasing the capacity of the Department as a unit which is a source of growth in the field of information technology that supports agricultural industrialization and other fields.
